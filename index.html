Revvyx – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Revvyx is a UK vehicle lookup app for iOS and Android. This policy explains what data is used, where it goes, and what is stored.

What Revvyx does

You enter a UK number plate. Revvyx looks up publicly available information about that vehicle from official government APIs and third-party automotive databases, then displays it to you.

To do this, the app talks to the Revvyx backend — a server we operate that fetches the data on the app's behalf, caches results, generates AI content, and checks that requests come from a genuine copy of the app. This section explains what that server receives and keeps.

Data stored on your device

Most of your data lives only on your device:

WhatWhereWhy
Vehicles you save to GarageLocal app storageSo you can track MOT/tax dates
Vehicles you add to FavouritesLocal app storageSo you can compare and revisit them
App settings (theme, units, reminders)Local storageTo remember your preferences
Cached lookup resultsLocal app storageTo avoid repeated lookups

You can clear cached data at any time from the app's Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all data stored on your device.

Data processed by the Revvyx backend

When you look up a vehicle, the app sends the request to the Revvyx backend. The backend:

WhatDetailWhy
The registration you searchThe number plate is sent to the backend and recordedTo fetch the vehicle data, cache it, and keep a record of searches to operate, secure and improve the app
The vehicle details returnedMake, model, year, fuel, colour, tax/MOT status, CO₂, engine size, mileageProduct analytics — to understand which vehicles are looked up and improve the service
Approximate locationYour country, derived from your IP address by our hosting providerRegional analytics and abuse prevention
PlatformWhether the request came from iOS or AndroidAnalytics and support
Device integrity checkAn attestation token from Apple (DeviceCheck) or Google (Play Integrity)To confirm the request comes from a genuine, untampered copy of the app and block abuse. We do not receive your Apple or Google account details from this.

Retention. Vehicle results are cached on the backend for up to about 7 days (technical specifications, which don't change, may be kept longer). Search analytics are kept for roughly 3 months and then expire automatically. We do not create accounts, so this data is not tied to your name or email.

IP addresses. Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) processes your IP address to route requests, derive your country, and rate-limit abusive traffic. We do not store your IP address ourselves.

Legal basis (UK GDPR). A UK registration mark can, in some cases, identify the registered keeper, so we treat it as personal data. We process the data above under our legitimate interests in operating, securing, analysing and improving the app. You have the right to access or request deletion of data we hold — contact us at the address below.

Our backend also relies on Cloudflare for hosting, caching and security, and on Apple and Google for the device-integrity checks described above.

We do not control how these third parties handle the data they receive. Please refer to their respective privacy policies. Anthropic (Claude): only vehicle data and any price you enter is sent — never your name, contact details, or location. See anthropic.com/privacy.

What Revvyx does NOT do

  • Does not create user accounts or ask for your name, email, or phone number
  • Does not track you across other apps or websites
  • Does not use advertising SDKs or sell your data to anyone
  • Does not record your precise location

Notifications

If you enable MOT or tax reminders, Revvyx schedules local notifications on your device based on the expiry dates of vehicles in your Garage. These are generated and delivered entirely on your device — no data is sent to a server to schedule them. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
